Wellcome to my personal page!

I am a research engineer in Machine Learning & Computer Vision. I obtained my engineering degree from the Institut National des Sciences Appliquées de Centre Val de Loire (Blois, France) in 2016, later I started my Ph.D. in Computer Vision and Machine Learning at Université de Tours (Tours, France). The topic of my thesis was to develop a collaborative multi-camera tracking system, which was part of a research project, named LUMINEUX - or 'luminous' in English, aiming to create a smart city with a system of intelligent sensors for smart street lighting, traffic control, etc. After finished my PhD in 2020. I joined a start-up, named XXII Group, based at Puteaux (next to Paris), France to develop a SmartCity platform, which analyses camera videos in real-time. The system are deployed in multiple cites across France, Spain and the US.
